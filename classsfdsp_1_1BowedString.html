Detailed Description

Implements a bowed string model using a waveguide composed of a right traveling wave and a left traveling wave.

Member Enumeration Documentation

◆ ParamId

enum class sfdsp::BowedString::ParamId
strong

Modifiable parameters for the bowed string model.

Enumerator
Velocity 

The velocity of the bow. 0 the bow is not moving, 1 the bow is moving at full speed.

Force 

The force of the bow. 0 the bow is not touching the string, 1 the bow is applying full force.

BowPosition 

The position of the bow. 0 the bow is at the bridge, 1 the bow is at the nut.

FingerPressure 

The finger pressure. 0 the finger is not touching the string, 1 the finger is applying full pressure. Values between 0 and 0.1 can result in harmonics.

NutGain 

The gain (loss) of the nut. At 0, all the energy is lost at the nut. At 1, all the energy is reflected back.

BridgeFilterCutoff 

The cutoff frequency of the bridge filter. At 0, the cutoff is at a normalized frequency of 0.025. At 1, the cutoff is at 0.125.

Changing the bridge filter can slightly detune the string. Use the tuning adjustment parameter to manually tune the string. Not recommended for real time use.

TuningAdjustment 

Used to fine tune the frequency of the string. At 0, 5 samples of delay are removed from the string. At 1, 5 samples of delay are added.
