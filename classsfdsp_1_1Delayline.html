Construct a delayline.

Parameters
max_sizeThe maximum size of the delayline in samples.
reverseIf true, TapIn() and TapOut() will access the delayline in reverse. See note.
interpolation_typeThe interpolation type to use.
Note
In a canonical waveguide structure, you have a delayline going in one direction and a delayline going in the other. For example, to access the rightmost samples of the delayline, you would use TapOut(0) on the right traveling line and TapOut(end) on the left traveling one. When reverse is True, the delayline will be accessed in reverse which allows you to access the two delaylines with the same index.
